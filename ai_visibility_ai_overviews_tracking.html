Recommended action rules

Use these rules to avoid treating AI visibility as magic. Measure, segment, then act.

AI Overview: Yes, mentioned: Yes

Protect and improve. Strengthen cited URL, keep content fresh, monitor competitor displacement.

AI Overview: Yes, mentioned: No

Main opportunity. Study cited sources and improve content structure, entity clarity and trust signals.

AI Overview: No, high intent

Classic SEO still matters most. Build rankings, content and commercial landing page quality.

AI Overview: Yes, no clear links

High click risk. Track CTR separately and focus on brand/entity reinforcement.

Step-by-step workflow

1. Define query set

Build category query clusters: transactional, commercial investigation, informational-commercial and brand-modified queries.

2. Capture SERP state

Track whether AI Overviews appear, which sources are cited and whether the marketplace is mentioned.

3. Join SEO data

Connect AI observations with GSC clicks, impressions, CTR, rankings, URL type and category value.

4. Score AI visibility

Calculate presence, mention rate, citation frequency, entity strength, schema coverage and review signal score.

5. Diagnose gaps

Separate ranking gaps, content gaps, citation gaps, structured data gaps, review gaps and entity gaps.

6. Prioritize work

Use business value, fixability, AI Overview presence and effort to rank initiatives.

7. Validate after changes

Compare before/after by stable query cluster. Do not judge by one query screenshot.

8. Report uncertainty

Show confidence level. AI visibility is volatile, so dashboard confidence matters as much as the score.
